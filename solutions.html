What We Do.

Five capability areas, designed to plug into each other. Most engagements start in one area and expand from there as the business grows.

Internal Tools Workflow Automation System Integrations AI Agents Data & Reporting
01 · Headline capability

Internal Tools & Dashboards.

The custom admin panels, ops consoles and back-office apps that sit at the centre of how your business actually runs. Built around your real workflow — not someone else's template — and made to last.

Operations dashboards

One screen for the team running the day-to-day: jobs in flight, exceptions, SLAs, anything that needs a human eye.

Custom CRMs & client records

When generic CRM doesn't fit — bespoke client, matter, tenancy or job records that match how your business actually thinks.

Quote, order & invoice tools

End-to-end quote → order → invoice → reconcile workflows, with approvals, templates and audit history built in.

Client / supplier portals

Secure external portals so clients and suppliers can self-serve status, documents and approvals — without emailing your team.

Internal review & approval flows

Multi-step approvals (timesheets, expenses, contracts, content) with proper roles, queues and notifications.

Replacing spreadsheet processes

The "load-bearing spreadsheet" no one wants to touch — rebuilt as a proper internal tool with permissions, history and validation.

02

Workflow Automation.

Removing the repetitive, rules-based work that's currently sitting in someone's inbox or copy-paste loop. Reliable, monitored, with clear human handover points where they're needed.

Lead / enquiry routing

Capture, enrich, qualify and route inbound enquiries automatically — into the right CRM stage and the right person's queue.

Document generation

Contracts, statements, proposals and reports generated from your data — branded, accurate, on demand.

Onboarding & off-boarding flows

Client, employee or supplier onboarding orchestrated end-to-end: requests, approvals, account creation, document collection.

Recurring billing & reconciliation

Automated invoice generation, payment chasing, reconciliation against bank feeds and accounting platforms.

Notification & alerting

The right person notified the moment a number, status or threshold needs human attention — via email, Slack, Teams or SMS.

Scheduled background jobs

Reliable scheduled tasks: nightly data syncs, weekly reporting, monthly close — with monitoring, retries and audit logs.

03

System Integrations.

Connecting the tools you already pay for, so your data lives in one place and stays in sync. No more "we entered it in the CRM but it never made it to accounting".

CRM ↔ accounting

Two-way sync between your CRM (HubSpot, Salesforce, Pipedrive, bespoke) and accounting (Xero, QuickBooks, Sage).

Scheduling ↔ ops ↔ payroll

Field scheduling and time tracking flowing into ops dashboards and through to payroll without manual re-entry.

Form & website → backend

Website enquiries, intake forms and booking flows wired directly into your real systems — properly validated.

Payment & banking

Stripe, GoCardless, open banking feeds reconciled against invoices, statements and customer records.

Comms platforms

Slack, Teams, Twilio, email — used as proper alert and workflow channels, not just chat.

Custom & legacy systems

The supplier portal with no real API, the legacy on-prem system, the partner sending CSVs — we deal with those too.

04 · Practical, not theatrical

AI Agents & Assistants.

LLM-based features built into your real processes, properly scoped and supervised. We use AI where it pays for itself — not because it sounds impressive in a slide deck.

Inbox triage & drafting

Incoming email read, categorised, routed, and (optionally) draft replies prepared for human approval.

Document extraction

Pulling structured data out of PDFs, invoices, contracts and forms — into your real systems, reviewed where it matters.

Summarisation & briefings

Long threads, call transcripts and case histories summarised into the briefing your team actually needs.

Internal "ask the company" search

An internal assistant trained on your own documentation, policies and processes — so the answer isn't always "ask Sarah".

Quality and compliance review

First-pass review of outputs (proposals, reports, candidate submissions) against your own quality criteria, with humans in the loop.

Conversational interfaces

Internal chat-style interfaces over your real data — for ops staff who shouldn't have to learn a dashboard to ask a question.

05

Data & Reporting.

One trustworthy source of truth across the business. Clean pipelines, live dashboards, and the numbers leadership needs to make confident decisions — without month-end spreadsheet stitching.

Unified data warehouse

Operational, financial and customer data consolidated into one well-modelled source of truth.

Leadership dashboards

The handful of numbers that actually matter — revenue, margin, utilisation, pipeline — live, accurate, and shared.

Operational dashboards

Real-time views for the teams running jobs, projects or accounts — exceptions, SLAs, queue lengths, ageing.

Scheduled reporting

Weekly / monthly reports built, branded and delivered automatically — to clients, partners, the board.

Data quality monitoring

Alerts when the numbers stop making sense — duplicates, gaps, anomalies — before they reach the board pack.

Reporting-as-a-service

For teams that need the reports without standing up an in-house data function. We own the pipeline.
